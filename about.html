Our Story

Designed for the Woman Who Moves

Born from a belief that activewear should be as refined as the women who wear it.

Elvora began with a simple question: why should activewear compromise on elegance? We believe the women who move through the world with purpose deserve clothing that matches their standard.

Our collections are crafted for padel, pilates, tennis, training, and every wellness ritual in between. Each piece is designed with intention — fabric that performs, silhouettes that flatter, and a finish that feels luxurious.
